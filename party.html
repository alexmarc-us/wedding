The Cocktail Party

Beer, wine and hand crafted cocktails will be served throughout the night. A TBD food truck will also be there providing small bites though not a full meal. If you are the hangry type, please eat beforehand.

9:00 p.m. - Alex and Chelsea enjoy their first dance.

10:00 p.m. - Cake! Toasts!

11:00 p.m. - Those with a taste for the Capitol Hill nightlife are invited to start bar hopping with the Bride and Groom.
