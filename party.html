The Cocktail Party

October 6th at 8:00pm

Alex and Chelsea would like to invite you to celebrate the start of their married lives together at a small cocktail party in Seattle. The event space is OOLA Distillery. Beer, wine and hand crafted cocktails will be served throughout the night. There will be some small bites provided though not a full meal. If you are the hangry type, please eat beforehand.

The details are being worked out, but the current party timeline is:

8:00 p.m. - Please enjoy some handmade to order cocktails, local craft brews or wine of indeterminate quality. Chow down on some hearty nibbles.

9:00 p.m. - Alex and Chelsea enjoy their first dance.

10:00 p.m. - Cake! Toasts!

11:00 p.m. - Those with a taste for the Capitol Hill nightlife are invited to start bar hopping with the Bride and Groom.
