The Wedding

October 6th at 11:00am

Alex and Chelsea will be married at a small chapel in Port Gamble, WA - a small town on the Kitsap Peninsula near where they live.

As one of their closest family and friends, they wanted to invite you to experience the beauty of where they live as well as be present to witness their big day.

These directions assume you are taking your own car, but carpooling is encouraged. If you have space or need a ride, note that in your RSVP.

Here’s how to get there:

  1. Arrive in Seattle (or live there already!)
  2. Rent a car (or own one already!)
  3. Get on the Bainbridge Island ferry
  4. Snap some sweet ferry photos
  5. Navigate to St. Paul’s Church in Port Gamble, WA
  6. Follow the signs for parking

Please dress in whatever attire makes you most comfortable.

If you make the trip out for Alex and Chelsea’s ceremony, you will have plenty of time to explore the area and still make it back to Seattle by 8:00 p.m. for their cocktail party. A few suggestions:

  • Take a hike at Port Gamble Forest Heritage Park
  • Visit Kingston or Port Townsend
  • Go for a beautiful drive up to Dungeness Spit
  • Eat lunch at the Butcher and Baker
